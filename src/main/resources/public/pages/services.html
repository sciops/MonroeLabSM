A simpler alternative

MonroeBox is a one-way Bitcoin dispenser. Full featured Bitcoin ATMs already exist on the market, but they are expensive, require an Internet connection, and are complicated to maintain. MonroeBox is designed to be simple, with no installation required. Just plug it in and deposit Bitcoin to your private keys.



Embedded private key generation

MonroeBox generates private keys from pre-determined seed values. This allows the operator to load Bitcoin onto the addresses without having the dispenser connected to the Internet. Being an air-gapped, connectionless device means that your financial security is not complicated by another third-party. You can use your own Bitcoin wallet app to maintain your funds.
